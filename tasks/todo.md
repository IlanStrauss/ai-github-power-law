# GitHub Concentration Analysis - TODO

## COMPLETED ANALYSIS

### Main Findings
- Dramatic concentration increase: Top 1% share went from 49.5% (2019) to 68.9% (2024)
- Gini coefficient: 0.747 → 0.897
- Multi-repo filter confirms this is REAL, not just bots (45.3% → 63.9% among multi-repo devs)

### 2024 Explosion Investigation
- 180 accounts hit the 10,000 commit ceiling (vs 0 in 2019-2022, 1 in 2023)
- All top 20 accounts in 2024 have exactly 1 repo (automation)
- One account had 2.84 million commits before filtering

### AI Detection Results
- Used refined patterns: `aider:` prefix, `Co-authored-by: Copilot`, `generated by GPT/Claude`
- 2023: 50 AI commits (0.0008% of total)
- 2024: 115 AI commits (0.0015% of total)
- Most AI-assisted code has NO explicit markers
